Project Mission

Hawaiian Monk Seal Directory is a browsable / searchable seal ID directory for users to emotionally engage and connect with individual animals. Our aim is to boost community engagement, provide educational resources, and increase usage of the NOAA hotline for real-time seal sightings. Ultimately, we hope to bridge the gap between NOAA, citizen scientists, ocean enthusiasts, students, tourists, and the general public and cultivate a sense of shared stewardship for the conservation of Hawaiian monk seals.
